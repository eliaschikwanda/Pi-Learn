After completing your test, submit your answers using the submit button right at the end of the answering panel. Immediately Pii Learn will mark your test, tell you which questions you got wrong, grade your test according to the thresholds of that paper. If you want to keep record of that paper make sure record using the button on the same page.

Some questions have exam report comments. The comments are, sometimes, explantions on their respective question. You may find them useful in tackling a question you got wrong or that one you guessed.
